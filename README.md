The Crackdown: The crushing of student activism — from Mahmoud Khalil's detention and deportation proceedings to the weaponization of the McCarran-Walter Act against legal permanent residents for attending protests.
The Counter-Strategy: Concrete pillars for resistance — sovereign infrastructure, money exposure, cross-movement solidarity, divestment campaigns, legal resistance, and archiving everything.
